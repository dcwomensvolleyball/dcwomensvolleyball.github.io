League Commissioner: Katie Solook

DC Women's Volleyball is the premier weekly volleyball league in the District of Columbia!

We are a community of women with extensive volleyball experience who continue to play at a high level on a weekly basis. Many of our players played throughout college and continue to play in USAV, Play 4 Keeps, and other leagues/tournaments around the DMV.

League Details

  • National Cathedral School Underwood Athletic Center
  • Games on Tuesdays at 7 & 8PM
  • 8 week seasons
  • Next season starts early September, 2017
  • 6v6
  • Currently accepting individuals but please contact us if you have a full team
  • $550/team, individual cost varies by roster size

If you're interested in joining the league, please email dcwomensvolleyball@gmail.com with your name, volleyball background, preferred position(s), and availability!
