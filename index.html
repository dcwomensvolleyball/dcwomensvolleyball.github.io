DC Women's Volleyball

ANNOUNCEMENTS:

Now accepting players for the fall season!

The premier ladies' volleyball league in the District of Columbia!

League Commissioner: Katie Solook

DC Women's Volleyball is a community of women with extensive volleyball experience who continue to play at a high level on a weekly basis. Many of our players played throughout college and continue to play in USAV, Play 4 Keeps, and other leagues/tournaments around the DMV.

League Details

  • National Cathedral School Underwood Athletic Center
  • 6v6
  • Games on Tuesdays at 7 & 8 PM
  • 8 week seasons
  • The upcoming fall season's dates are 9/11, 9/18, 9/25, 10/2, 10/16, 10/23, 10/30, 11/6
  • The winter season's dates will be 1/8, 1/15, 1/22, 2/5, 2/12, 2/19, 2/26, 3/5
  • Around $550/team, individual cost varies by roster size

If you're interested in joining the league, please email dcwomensvolleyball@gmail.com with your name, volleyball background, preferred position(s), and availability!
