The premier ladies' volleyball league in the District of Columbia!

About DC Women's Volleyball: Founded in the fall of 2016, we are independently run with the goal of growing a competitive community for womenâ€™s volleyball in DC. Our average league member has experience playing competitive volleyball throughout high school and college (e.g., club, DIII, DII, DI).

Our league has the unique feature of having a captains' draft each season. This allows players more opportunity to play with different people and to continue/enhance their skill development (e.g., by trying different positions they may not have played in college), thus increasing competition.

League Founder & Commissioner: Katie Solook

Level of Play: This league is targeted for A level players and above. Elite high school, club, or college level player that can run different offenses (6-2, 5-1), have a great understanding of all positions on the court, and can execute all skills with accuracy and power. Few player errors and fast game play.

League Structure/Information: Women's 6s indoor. Participants sign up as individuals and teams are selected via captains' draft. Matches are on Tuesday evenings at 7 or 8pm.

Location: National Cathedral School, Underwood Athletic Center (see directions here)

Questions or interested in joining the league? Email dcwomensvolleyball@gmail.com with your name, volleyball background, preferred position(s), availability, and best contact method.
